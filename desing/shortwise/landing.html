// FAQ

Common questions.

Why not just use Figma?

You can — and many designers will. Shotwise is for the part of the work Figma doesn't do well: bulk-rendering nine locales at exact App Store dimensions, with AI-written copy and font-aware line breaking. Use Figma for the brand. Use Shotwise for the export.

How does the AI actually work?

We pass your app description and a low-res preview of each screenshot to a multimodal model that returns three title candidates per screen, in your chosen tone. You pick or rewrite. Nothing is auto-published.

Can I use my own brand colors and fonts?

Yes. The Brand Kit (Pro) stores your palette, type pairing, and logo. Wizard and Manual mode both pick them up automatically.

Does it work for Android / Play Store too?

Yes — Play Store presets (Phone, 7" tablet, 10" tablet) are first-class, side by side with App Store sizes.
