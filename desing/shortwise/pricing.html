Pricing questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — one click in the dashboard, no email required. Annual plans are pro-rated for refund within 14 days of renewal.

Do you offer refunds?

The 7-day Pro trial is free and cancelable. After that, we refund any full month on request within 30 days, no questions asked.

What payment methods?

Card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and SEPA — billed through LemonSqueezy, who handles VAT for EU customers.

Student or non-profit discount?

50% off Pro for students with an .edu address and for registered non-profits. Email us with proof.

What counts as an "export"?

One project ZIP, regardless of how many screens or languages are inside. Free is capped at 3 ZIPs per month.

What's coming next?

API access (Team) is live; Figma plugin, custom language packs, and SSO are in flight for Q3.

Free tier is free forever.

Three exports a month. One language. Zero commitment.
